The Problem

Your AI writes code fast.
But then…

Without structure, without memory, without a system — it creates chaos just as quickly. Here's why that happens and how to fix it.

The Problem

What goes wrong with AI coding

Every non-technical builder hits the same walls when they start shipping with AI.

🎨

Inconsistent design

The AI designs differently every session. Your brand looks like three different companies stitched together.

💥

Fix one, break five

No tests, no safety net. Every fix introduces two new bugs. You redo the same work week after week.

🐟

Goldfish memory

The agent forgets everything between sessions. Your stack, your decisions, your context — gone in a new chat.

🪂

No safety net

Zero tests. Zero docs. Zero guardrails. You ship and pray. One bad deploy and you're back to square one.

🍜

Skill soup

You install 15 skills from different authors. None talk to each other. They overlap, conflict, and create noise.

AI gave me 100 hands. Without discipline, those hands created chaos.
