## Phase 4: Evaluate & Recommend

**🌟 Recommendation: Choose Option A.**
Structure: **Turn ideas into [ ____________ ] 10x faster.**

**Why choose Option A?**
1. **Visual Power (Design):** Keeping "Turn ideas into..." fixed provides an anchor point for the user's eyes. When the final word is typed, it immediately pins what the software can do into their brain.
2. **Sharp & Concise (Content):** Words are only 2-4 words long: `production code`, `SaaS applications`, `real products`, `UX-standard designs`. Understandable at a glance, no fluff.
3. **UI Friendly (Tech):** Changing only one word avoids text jumping across different screen sizes (CLS - Cumulative Layout Shift), especially on mobile.
