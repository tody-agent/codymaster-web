KEY INSIGHT

Not Competitors — Complementary

🧪 What is Google AI Studio?

Google AI Studio is aweb-based prototyping environmentfor building with Gemini models. It's the "intelligence engine" — you test prompts, tune models, use multimodal inputs (text, images, video, audio), and export to production APIs.

🔗 aistudio.google.com

⚡ What is CodyMaster?

CodyMaster is a skill kit for AI coding agents. It doesn't provide AI models — it provides the professional methodology (TDD, deployment, security, UX) that makes any AI agent produce better code, including Gemini via AI Studio.

🔗 GitHub

Strengths & Limitations

🧪 Where AI Studio Excels

  • Most advanced Gemini models, first-party
  • 2M token context — massive codebase analysis
  • Multimodal: analyze images, video, audio
  • Deep Research agent for autonomous investigation
  • Model tuning without infrastructure

🧪 Where AI Studio Falls Short

  • Not an app builder — API/prototyping only
  • No deployment, no hosting, no database
  • Free tier shares your data with Google
  • No TDD, no quality gates, no security
  • No lifecycle management beyond prompting

⚡ Where CodyMaster Excels

  • Complete product lifecycle coverage
  • Works with Gemini AND other models
  • TDD, security, quality gates built-in
  • 100% private, 100% local
  • Marketing + UX + Content skills included

⚡ Where CodyMaster Falls Short

  • No AI model — depends on external agents
  • No multimodal capabilities
  • No model tuning or fine-tuning
  • Can't analyze video/audio/images
  • Not a prototyping playground
