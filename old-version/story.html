So What Is CodyMaster?

It's not a plugin. Not a prompt template. It's the answer from a Product Manager who can't code, after hundreds of sleepless nights, to one single question:

"How can someone with ideas but no technical skills create world-class products?"

Why "CodyMaster"?

🇻🇳

The Wordplay

"CodyMaster" comes from "Code Đi" (Cô-đi) — Vietnamese for "Go code!" An invitation to just start building, stop overthinking.

❤️

The Heart

Cody is also the name of the creator's child. This kit carries personal meaning — every skill was crafted with the same care and dedication as raising a child.

🏆

The Philosophy

CodyMaster = Code like an expert. Not just write code, but master the craft — with discipline, testing, planning, and quality at every step.
